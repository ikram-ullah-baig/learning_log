Success Secrets

Secrets of success are listed below:

  1. Stop thinking and start doing (Harkat mae barkat ha).
  2. Once you are off to a start there is no coming back(Kashtiyan Jala ka chalo).
  3. Calm water doesn't make a good sailor so hope for struggle and hardship which will build your character.
  4. "Commitment" and "Consistency" are the two driving wheels of Success.
  5. Make your own "LUCK".
